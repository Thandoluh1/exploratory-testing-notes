# Exploratory Test Charters

## Charter 1: Login Validation
- Explore login behavior with invalid, boundary, and unexpected inputs
- Focus on error handling and usability

## Charter 2: Checkout Flow
- Explore checkout process interruptions (page refresh, back button)
- Focus on data persistence and validation

## Charter 3: Contact Form
- Explore form behavior with incomplete or malformed data
- Focus on validation messages and UX consistency
